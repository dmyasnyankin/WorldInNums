What does this visualization mean?

  • The X-Axis (Horizontal Line) represents a Logarithmic Scale to account for increasing GDP per capita in countries.
  • The Y-Axis (Vertical Line) represents a Linear Scale to account for increasing Life Expectancy in countries.
  • Each circle corresponds to a different country, plotted relative to their life expectancy and GDP per capita for a given year.
  • The size of the circle represents the population of a given country in a particular point in time.
